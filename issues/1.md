### Roadmap
- **MVP (Weeks 1-4):** Landing + auth (homeowners/gardeners), service catalog, booking flow, availability, basic payments (Stripe test), job status updates, basic admin dashboard (users, pricing, manual payouts).
- **v1.1 (Weeks 5-8):** Ratings/reviews, in-app notifications, rescheduling, promo codes, payout automation, gardener earnings dashboard, calendar sync.
- **v2 (Weeks 9-12):** Route optimization, subscription plans, bundled services, in-app chat, before/after photos, dispute tooling.
